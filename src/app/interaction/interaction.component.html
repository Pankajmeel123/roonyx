What options do we have to establish such communication?,
For each option describe what are the pros and cons of this solution?,
For each option describe what are the cases the solution fits best?

1. Binding using @Input & @Output.
--- PROS:
------ It is the most basic way to communicate between components.
------ Binding is the cleanest and simplest one and most easily maintainable.
--- CONS:
------ Dont know any.
--- BEST USECASE:
------ Both way communication.

2. Reference using @ViewChild & @ContentChild.
--- PROS:
------ It is more easy and flexible way of communication.
--- CONS:
------ It is a problem for maintenance.
------ We cannot benefit from the Angular lifecycle hooks and we have to manage the state change manually.
--- BEST USECASE:
------ One way communication.

3. By creating Service.
--- PROS:
------ Dont need any linking between the components for communication.
--- CONS:
------ have to maintain large amount of code and an aditional service also put lode on the application.
--- BEST USECASE:
------ Communication b/w components with no relation.
